My Coding Journey

Tony Gonzales is a student with the UC Davis Boot Camp Program where he is spending the next 6 months immersed in learning both front end and back end web development. Tony is attending this school so that he can obtain a career in computer developmment in a challenging, ever changing environment.


Tony has worked in general and retail management for over 18 years and has experience using many avenues of technology in the workplace. He also has an inate ability to connect well with others and problem solve even the most difficult of things.
